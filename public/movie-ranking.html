My Top 3 Movies of All Time

The Godfather(1972)

A gripping tale of power, family, and organized crime, exploring the dark underbelly of the mafia.

The Way Way Back(2013)

A heartwarming coming-of-age story, following a shy teenager's transformative summer at a water park.

My Dinner with Andre (1981)

An intellectually stimulating and philosophical conversation between two friends during a thought-provoking dinner.
